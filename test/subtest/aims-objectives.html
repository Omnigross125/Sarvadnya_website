About Us

Welcome to Shri Krishna Yog Mandir, a sacred space for spiritual growth, devotion, and healing. Founded by Pankajraj Spiritual Guru, our center operates under the auspices of the Sarvadnya Mission Charitable Trust, with a mission to lead individuals toward the highest spiritual awakening and connection with the Divine.

Our goal is to cultivate deep Bhakti (devotion) to Lord Krishna and Shri Chakradhar Swami, while offering spiritual practices that elevate the body, mind, and soul. Our center provides a sanctuary for seekers of all walks of life who are dedicated to the sacred journey of self-realization, inner peace, and divine service.

Our Mission

We aim to help individuals walk the path of spiritual awakening, dedicated to the highest ideal of devotion and selfless service. Our core purpose is to inspire deep Bhakti to Lord Krishna and Shri Chakradhar Swami, guiding every individual to experience divine love, harmony, and transformation through sacred practices.

We are committed to creating a spiritual community where seekers are empowered to embark on their Oath of Spiritual Journey, embracing spiritual practices such as Yoga, Pranayama, Meditation, Seva, Sadhana, Satsang, Simran, Bhajan, and Healing.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a global community of like-minded souls committed to the Path of Spiritual Journey. By connecting with Lord Krishna and Shri Chakradhar Swami, we help individuals find their divine purpose, experience joy, and live with compassion and love.

We believe that spiritual practice is a way of life, and through devotion, service, and disciplined practice, one can achieve a state of higher consciousness, peace, and fulfillment.

Activities

  • Bhakti (Devotion to Lord Krishna and Shri Chakradhar Swami): The foundation of our practice is love and surrender to the Divine. Through Bhakti, we open our hearts to divine grace and develop a strong, unshakable connection with Lord Krishna and Chakradhar Swami.
  • Yoga: A holistic practice that harmonizes the body and mind, enhancing physical health and creating the space for spiritual awakening.
  • Pranayama (Breathing Techniques): Pranayama is an ancient science of breathing that purifies the mind and body, helping balance the life force (prana) within.
  • Meditation: Deep meditation practices to bring inner peace, clarity, and a heightened sense of spiritual awareness.
  • Seva (Selfless Service): We believe that true spiritual growth comes through selfless service to humanity. Our Seva programs are designed to help individuals purify their hearts and serve the greater good.
  • Sadhana (Spiritual Discipline): A disciplined approach to spiritual practices that guide individuals on their journey to self-realization and enlightenment.
  • Satsang (Spiritual Gathering): Regular gatherings where we come together to share wisdom, chant divine names, and engage in collective spiritual practices. These satsangs help us strengthen our connection to the divine and each other.
  • Simran: Simran is the practice of repeating the divine names of Lord Krishna and Chakradhar Swami to keep the mind focused on the Divine and to awaken a deep sense of divine presence.
  • Bhajan (Devotional Songs): Singing heartfelt devotional songs to express love, devotion, and gratitude towards the Divine, elevating the soul to higher states of consciousness.
  • Healing: We offer various spiritual healing techniques that help release emotional blockages, heal the body and mind, and restore balance to one's life.

Our Approach

At Shri Krishna Yog Mandir, we believe that the Path of Spiritual Journey is a commitment to living a life of devotion, service, and spiritual practice. This journey is one of continuous growth and inner transformation. Through the practice of Yoga, Bhakti, Simran, and Sadhana, we strive to walk the path of righteousness, peace, and divine love.

Our teachings are rooted in the sacred wisdom of Lord Krishna, the divine teachings of Chakradhar Swami, and ancient spiritual practices. We provide a supportive environment where every seeker can grow, learn, and progress on their spiritual path.

Join Us

Whether you are just beginning your spiritual journey or have been walking the path for years, Shri Krishna Yog Mandir welcomes you. Embrace the Path of Spiritual Journey, and allow us to guide you towards a life filled with love, peace, devotion, and divine fulfillment.
