CORPORATE YOGA WELNESS PROGRAM:


Yoga is a light. which once lit, will never dim, The better you practice, The brighter your flame! ~ BKS Iyengar

Welcome to our Corporate Yoga Wellness Program!!

In today's busy world where corporate employees usually have a hectic schedule leading to constant stress .. they truly need a solution which not only will give them physical fitness but mental well-being too. Our wellness program does just that for you!!

Mental stress in organizations lead to


  • Increase in medical ailments
  • Decreased efficiency and productivity
  • Lack of motivation and innovation
  • Poor time management
  • Rising attrition rates

In turn, if employees practice yoga, there are quite a few benefits:

Increased energy/ Reduced fatigue:


Simple Yoga techniques help in increasing blood circulation leading to better energy levels.

Increased Productivity and Creativity


Your body will most likely become much more flexible by doing yoga, and so will your mind. ~Cyndi Lee A balanced and healthy mind can work wonders for employees to step up and achieve more... and think out of the box!

Reduced physical ailments


Practice of yoga helps relieve even the most severe pains and physical problems. Every problem has an asana which can cure it!

How does our program help you?


  • Our asanas can be practiced by anyone, whatever your age.. age is definitely no bar here!
  • We concentrate on postures to relax every muscle and bring about elasticity and flexibility
  • Natural ways to ensure a relaxed body and mind
  • Helps in maintaining a balanced metabolism
  • Helps in improving your skin and bring a natural glow.
  • Helps in strengthening the immune system
  • Helps in lowering the stress levels, both physical and mental
