Some Story Behind Us

Yoga is the journey of the self, through the self, to the self.

Welcome to The Sarvadnya School of Yoga, founded by Sri Pankaj Raj Bidkar in 2010. We are here to help you to become a better person with own spiritual growth.

Various household’s and Sanyasi’s who loves and willingly serves the society without anything in return. We believe yoga is the best way to serve to humanity, to teach others and to understand yourself in a better way!

We at The Sarvadnya School of Yoga aim to bestow the spiritual world of Yoga to the people who willing to dive into the powerful mind, body healing & growth. Yoga make you understand the way of enriching your spiritual, mental and educational knowledge in a sorted way. Transforming your stressful life to peaceful place needs you to have proper environment, people and all -round development under guidance of right people. Our mission is to enhance society to discover many hidden secrets in the physical realm of our existence through Power of Yoga.

We believe that every individual in society are associated with different life values, religious beliefs and interests, then with a little you can learn yoga in addition with the true meaning of life. We stress on developing meaningful discipline in your own spiritual journey, which can be passed to the society in good manner.

Sarvadnya Mission Charitable trust is a Non-Profit organization undertakes following programs like Ancient Yoga Principle’s, the importance of meditation, Incorporation Yoga with breathing practices to lead an individual to acquire positive vibes and happiness in day to day life routine. And the best part of training is opting it your home in your own comfort zone.

Sarvadnya yoga aims to connect with people and guides them to the core of life, helps you elevate the level of consciousness among you. We always value your emotional investment of time, energy and with us. Get in touch with our Yoga Masters who will not only motivate you but inspire you through their experience. As yoga is powerful vehicle for change, as you build strength to believe that you are gaining more potential.
