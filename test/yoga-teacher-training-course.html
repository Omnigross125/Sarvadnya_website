YOGA TEACHER TRAINING COURSE - 200 HOURS


•Yoga is a Nobel path to gain greatest wisdom in your life. 200 hours’ yoga training India, is basically an experience which gives intensive yoga practice of traditional yoga’s such as hatha yoga in a soulful and peaceful environment. The course is basically divided into two modules where one half is to train new teachers where they learn new methodologies of the asanas in detailed practices. While the other half of the course is about improving Meditation, Pranayama’s and practical philosophy which combines spiritual & yogic aspects all together. •Yoga teachers training can mislead you as you can say it is only for teachers why are taking training but it can also help people who want transformational shift in their body, to have breathing awareness and to have meaningful purpose in life. •In this modern crazy world, we need real detoxification for our body all the time. And to fulfill this purpose and to live same peaceful life, 200 hours’ yoga training and techniques will help you have physical and intellectual knowledge in professional way.

What are Yoga Teaching Methodologies?


The following methodologies are practiced in the yoga sessions. Just take a look at it:

  • Yoga sequence creation lessons
  • Sessions for techniques to teach proper principles of Yoga
  • Methods to Manage Yoga Classes
  • Practical Workshops and demonstrations
  • Skills trainings and guidelines for assisting, motivating the students
  • Plans to be good ethical yoga teacher
  • Improving communication skills of trainers
  • Testing & sharing skills to co-teachers

    Anatomy means basic knowledge about whole body system which we be taught by us in the sessions. Here we will learn about the functions of various organs, glands and other systems which are affected and needs healing to avoid more pain and injuries.

    Service (Seva):


    Students will do group activities like serving meals, cleaning during their yoga teacher training course. The community work during yoga teacher training course will make them aware of the yeal meaning of Yoga i.e. Seva.

    Basic Sanskrit:


    Yoga is ancient as the scriptures are in Sanskrit. The basic yoga lessons will be introduced to you. The lessons include Yoga, Sutra’s and Mantra recitation.

    Basic Teachings of the Bhagavad-Gita:


    Our Sanskrit lessons will give you a basic introduction to the language of Sanskrit, the Sanskrit words most commonly used in yoga, the sutras and mantra recitation.

    Basic Teachings of the Bhagavad-gita:


    You will be learning basic about the history of Bhagavad Gits, which will help you for self – discovery, and will show you how to apply it in your normal life.
