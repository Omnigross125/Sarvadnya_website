Some Story Behind Us

We Have 10 Years of experince

Acharya Pankaj Raj Bidkar is the founder of Sarvandya Mission which is known as well-established a Non-profit Spiritual, Social, Educational and Medical Organization.

Our Mission

The Sarvadnya Yoga Mission believes in creating health awareness among all living beings in society with sum of mental, physical, emotional, social economical & spiritual well-being. Mission provides holistic health environment among society.

Our Vision

Yoga propagates ancient yet great wisdom through scientific methods which can bring light of hope through everyone. We want every single individual in society to have healthy way of living through the yoga practices.
